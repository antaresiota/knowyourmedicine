Digestive System

Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)
Esomeprazole (Nexium), lansoprazole (Prevacid), omeprazole (Prilosec, Zegerid), pantoprazole (Protonix), rabeprazole (Aciphex) and dexlansoprazole (Dexilant).
Peptic Ulcer Disease (PUD) and Gastritis
Proton pump inhibitors (also called PPIs ) These drugs include the prescription and over-the-counter medications omeprazole (Prilosec), lansoprazole (Prevacid), rabeprazole (Aciphex), esomeprazole (Nexium) and pantoprazole (Protonix)
Stomach Flu
promethazine, prochlorperazine, metoclopramide, ondansetron,loperamide hydrochloride (Imodium) or bismuth subsalicylate (Pepto-Bismol).
Inflammatory Bowel Disease
anti-inflammatories include corticosteroids and aminosalicylates, such as mesalamine (Asacol HD, Delzicol, others), balsalazide (Colazal) and olsalazine (Dipentum).
Irritable Bowel Syndrome
pyridoindole in Alosetron ,silicon dioxide, disodium edetate, glycerol palmitostearate in rifaxamin
Constipation
Docusate and Polyethylene glycol
Hemorrhoids
phenylephrine, lidocaine, or hydrocortisone
Diverticular Disease
Ciprofloxacin plus metronidazole,Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole plus metronidazole or Amoxicillin-clavulanate.
Gallstones
Ursodiol
